Square checkout

Pay online

Use the secure checkout page to pay with a debit card, credit card, or other supported methods available through Square.

  • Best for invoice and quote payments
  • Processed securely by Square
  • Confirmation is provided after payment is completed
Pay now

Available methods may include: debit and credit cards, Cash App Pay, Afterpay, Apple Pay, and Google Pay depending on device and browser.

Direct Cash App

Optional direct payment

If preferred, payment can also be sent directly to $HolfeSolutions through Cash App.

  • Useful if you already pay through Cash App
  • Receipts can be provided on request
Open Cash App

Important: direct Cash App payments do not appear inside Square invoices or reports.
