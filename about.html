About HolFe Solutions

A trusted support practice, evolved for the AI era.

HolFe Solutions grew out of decades of hands-on technical support, practical troubleshooting, and direct client care. Today that same grounded approach extends into AI readiness, digital operations, and workflow guidance for small businesses that want to modernize without losing control.

The founder

Juan Carlos Holguin

For more than 35 years, Juan Carlos has worked across the practical side of technology: repair, support, software and workflow help, business troubleshooting, and high-trust client service. That long view matters now more than ever, because modern technology choices affect not only devices, but how a business communicates, documents work, and makes decisions.

HolFe Solutions brings that experience to owners who want guidance that is calm, clear, and grounded in reality.

Then

Personalized support with a human touch

The business began by helping households, professionals, and small offices solve the everyday technology problems that slow life and work down.

  • Device support and office troubleshooting
  • Networking, migration, and systems cleanup
  • Patient communication in English and Spanish
Now

Digital operations and AI readiness

That same trust-based service model now supports a broader need: helping businesses modernize their processes and adopt AI with maturity and care.

  • Workflow mapping and modernization planning
  • Administrative optimization and systems guidance
  • Responsible adoption of AI tools for real business use

How HolFe works

  • Respect budgets, pace, and the realities of small business.
